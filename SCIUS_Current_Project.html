SCIUS TU-SKR

The Science Classrooms in University-Affiliated School Project

This platform is created by M5 students from the SCIUS project (Thammasat University and Suankularb Wittayalai Rangsit School). Because our science and math curriculum is intense, we built this comedy news platform to laugh off the academic stress and document our daily campus survival.

Official Node: scius-tu.com

Our Editorial Standards

  • No Fake News: We verify all timelines via LINE timestamps and Google Drive before publishing.
  • Two-Sided Confrontation: If a group leader complains, we interview the accused partner to ensure fairness.
  • Bias Policy: verified facts are presented neutrally. Our dramatic reactions are clearly labeled as "Host's Opinion" solely for comedy.
